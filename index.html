About us

Founded by volunteers, driven by outcomes

Lets Work For Bangladesh was started by nine Bangladeshi-Australians working together to change the lives of people in Bangladesh — especially through education. For nearly a decade, we've channelled the generosity of our community into real projects on the ground.

🎯

Not for income, but for outcome

No offices, no salaried staff, no administration costs. We are run by elected volunteers so that your donation reaches the people who need it.

🤝

Trusted local partners

We deliver through trusted partners in Bangladesh, enabling reliable, transparent implementation right at the ground level.

🔍

Transparent & accountable

Committee members travel to remote areas of Bangladesh to monitor projects directly and report back to donors.

🌱

Basic needs for all

Our vision is a Bangladesh where everyone has access to the basics — food, health, shelter and education.
